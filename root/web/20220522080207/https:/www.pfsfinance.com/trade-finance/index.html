Trade Finance

We are a facilitator of “Direct” corporate loans and have an appealing track record of closing project finance facilities extended by OPIC and trade finance facilities insured or guaranteed by the Export Import Bank of the United States of America (“US Eximbank”) and other export credit agencies as applicable.
We identify potential loan transactions, evaluate credit risks and review other factors in making our loans. The loans are funded directly by our Principals whom retain full control over notes servicing, documentation, guarantees and claims.

Demonstrated Track-Record

Leading and executing floating or fixed rate loan facilities extended to importers of capital goods and services.
