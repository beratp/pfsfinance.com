About Us

PFS Finance (USA), LLC is a boutique financial services company headquartered in New York, NY.
The firm provides clients with access to;

  • medium- to long-term funding through direct loans,
  • structured finance facilities,
  • mergers, acquisitions and dispositions,
  • refinancing and recapitalizations, and
  • other financial advisory services.
