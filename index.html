About PFS Finance

Established in 2010, we are a specialized financial advisory partnership that serves as a vehicle for medium- to long-term ECA/Agency-backed trade, corporate and project finance transactions.

Our Role

We serve as an "Originator" under agreements with leading international Lenders and Agencies, offering financial solutions aligned with their capabilities and tailored to specific requirements.

Who We Are

PFS Finance (USA), LLC is a boutique financial services company headquartered in New York, NY. We are a specialized financial advisory partnership that serves as a vehicle for medium- to long-term ECA/Agency-backed trade, corporate and project finance transactions.

Our company operates as an "Originator" under agreements with leading international Lenders and Agencies ("Principals"), offering financial solutions that align with their capabilities and are tailored to specific requirements. We serve as independent contractors, maintaining arm's length relationships while providing comprehensive support from financial due diligence to operational follow-up.

Our Approach

We conduct comprehensive KYC checks and meet all regulatory requirements, including OFAC and prohibited parties screening. Our disciplined approach to risk assessment and management ensures we can be reliable partners through all market conditions.

Client Relationships

We build long-term relationships based on trust, transparency, and mutual success rather than transactional interactions. Our portfolio includes nearly 200 client contacts representing over $2 billion in financing offers extended to date.
